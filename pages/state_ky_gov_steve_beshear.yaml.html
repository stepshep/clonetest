    Appearance Request

    To request an appearance by Governor Steve Beshear, please complete and submit the Appearance Request Form.

    Forms can also be mailed or faxed to:

    Elizabeth Roach
    Office of the Governor
    700 Capitol Avenue, Suite 112
    Frankfort, KY 40601

    Fax: (502) 564-0437

    Due to the fluidity of the Governor’s schedule, appearances are not confirmed more than two to three weeks in advance.

    If you have any questions, please contact Elizabeth Roach at (502) 564-2611.
