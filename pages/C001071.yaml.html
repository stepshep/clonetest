We deeply value our correspondence with Tennesseans. To send an email to Senator Corker regarding legislation in Congress or his work in the U.S. Senate, please fill out the contact form below.

If you need casework assistance with a federal agency or are inquiring about federal grants, flag requests, internships, service academy nominations, tour requests, schedule request, or visiting Washington, D.C., please select the appropriate topic from the “Services for Tennesseans” drop down menu.

For meeting requests and all additional inquiries, please call our D.C. office at 202-224-3344. To invite Senator Corker to an event in Tennessee, please contact our Chattanooga office at 423-756-2757.

If you need to send documents to Senator Corker, please fax them to our D.C. office at 202-228-0566.

You can also send correspondence by mail to our D.C. office, but please note there will be a significant delay in receipt of your letter due to required security screening of all postal items addressed to Senator Corker.

All mail should be sent to:
